Devara

Directed by Koratala Siva, 'Devara' will release in in two parts. This grand project is produced by Yuvasudha Arts and NTR Arts, and is presented by Nandamuri Kalyan Ram. The film features NTR Jr in the lead role, with Janhvi Kapoor and Saif Ali Khan also starring in significant roles.
